About

In the project Riotcat.org, we are trying to provide various open-source web services and applications to enable friends to interact. In addition to the existing projects, we are also writing our own programs or plugins. The blog will provide information on current news or interesting information about servers, open-source and so on.
Services are only accessible to a limited number of people due to limited resources.

Privacy Policy

In this document we want to describe what data is processed and stored on riotcat.org and what its purpose is.

If you have any further questions or problems, please feel free to contact us. You can find our contact details here or write an email to info@riotcat.org.

Information Used

Our goal is to store and use only necessary information, and to store it only for the period of time needed.

On Riotcat.org, we do not use cookies.

For maintenance reasons, logfiles are generated when you connect to our website.

The logfile contains: an anonymized IP address (the last number is always set to 0, e.g. 78.46.95.0), a timestamp, requested resource, http referrer URL, byte size of the response, and the HTTP status code.

After 2 days, the log files are automatically deleted.

Temporary exceptions are possible to secure our services against attacks and for debugging purposes.

Connection (TLS) and encryption

We enforce a secure transport channel by providing only an encrypted HTTPS connection. You can check the status via our browser icon (usually a padlock icon).

All our hard drives are encrypted.

Location

Our server is located inside the EU. Implied rights and obligations of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) apply here.
